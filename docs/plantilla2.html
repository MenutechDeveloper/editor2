About Solstice Energy

Why Solar Now?

Energy prices are volatile — solar provides predictable long-term energy costs, reduces exposure to grid price increases and often qualifies for tax credits and rebates. Our designs are tailored to maximize return on investment and meet corporate ESG goals.

How We Work

From feasibility and permitting to procurement, construction and commissioning — we manage every stage. Our preferred equipment list includes Tier 1 PV modules and smart inverters for reliability and performance monitoring.
